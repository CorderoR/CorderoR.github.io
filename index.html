My (Unique) Pathway

I left college at 20. A decade of leading large-scale teams, plus great mentors and a lot of grit eventually led me to Yale University.

At Yale I learned to bridge rigorous math and CS with pragmatic program management. That work drew the attention of the Yale School of Management, where I joined the inaugural Master's in Technology Management cohort. We tackled real-world problems behind today’s AI initiatives.

Today my work spans AI/ML, LLMs, platforms, and data products. I own strategy, roadmaps, and delivery with research, engineering, and business stakeholders.

I value clarity, iteration, and outcomes, but above all, relationships.
