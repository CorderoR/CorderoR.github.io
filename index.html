My (Unique) Pathway

After a decade leading large-scale teams and managing complex operations across global markets, I recognized that the future of business execution lies at the intersection of technical precision and strategic leadership. This drove me to pursue formal technical education at Yale University to deepen my expertise in AI and systems design.

At Yale I learned to bridge rigorous Mathematics and Computer Science with pragmatic program management. That work drew the attention of the Yale School of Management, where I joined the inaugural Master's in Technology Management cohort. We tackled real-world problems behind today’s AI initiatives.

Today my work spans AI/ML, LLMs, platforms, and data products. I own strategy, roadmaps, and delivery with research, engineering, and business stakeholders. I value clarity, iteration, and outcomes, but above all, relationships.
